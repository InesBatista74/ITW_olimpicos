History of the Olympic Games

The Olympic Games are a universal symbol of competition, overcoming and unity among people. Bringing together athletes from all over the world, the event celebrates sporting excellence and promotes values ​​such as respect, friendship and equality. More than a sporting event, the Games represent the meeting of cultures and the search for achievements that inspire generations.
