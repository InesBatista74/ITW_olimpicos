About us and our work

We, Guilherme Martins and Inês Batista, are software engineering students from the University of Aveiro, developers of this same website which takes as its theme the 2024 Summer Olympic Games. This project was developed within the scope of ITW, for JSP, using the components learned during the semester in his classes.

This project consists of an informative website about the 2024 Summer Olympic Games, with information on various fields, namely athletes, modalities, participating countries, Olympic torch routes, the history of the Olympic Games, among others, with a lot to offer, so you you should explore it!

In building the website, all the knowledge acquired during the semester's ITW classes was applied, such as the search bar, maps, graphs, carousel, lots of bootstrap, use of APIs, shopping cart, forms...
